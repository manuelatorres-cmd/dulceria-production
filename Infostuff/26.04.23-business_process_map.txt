# Dulceria — How the Business Runs

*Reference map — generated 2026-04-23. End-to-end view of how Dulceria operates, independent of the app implementation. Pair with the code only when building a new feature.*

## 0. At a glance
- **What:** Handmade vegan chocolate praline production in Vienna.
- **Sells through 4 channels:** B2B, Events, Online shop, Physical store.
- **Produces via:** Multi-step batches (9 steps; bars skip 1–2), mould-constrained, people-constrained.
- **Tracks:** Products (pieces) + Ingredients (grams) + Packaging (units) + Fillings (grams).
- **Non-negotiable:** HACCP — daily temperature logs, cleaning checklists.

## 1. Catalog hierarchy
```
Ingredients (g) → Fillings (g, versioned) → Products (pieces) → Variants (SKUs: product+packaging+price) → Collections (label groupings, derived)
```
- Products: default mould, shell %, fillings, production steps, shelf life, decoration design, allergens.
- Fillings: fresh OR shelf-stable (water-activity-tracked) OR frozen/reused (FIFO).
- Moulds: physical inventory — **the real bottleneck**. 60 praline + 25 bar. Locked from Polishing to Unmoulding.
- Packaging: boxes/inserts/ribbons. Own low-stock thresholds + lead times.
- Ingredients: stock in grams, consumed at Shelling + Filling steps, FIFO.
- Variants: `kind` = curated (fixed products per box, locked on order) or free-pick. Sizes = packagings with per-channel price overrides (b2b/shop/event/online).
- Labels on variants → Collections page.

## 2. Who we sell to
- **B2B** (hotels/pastry shops/retailers) — negotiated prices, quotes, branded packaging, longer lead times.
- **Private** (retail/walk-ins/online/events).
- Customer record: company, contact, VAT number, payment terms, delivery method, invoice address, allergen notes, packaging prefs, language, default price list (a variant), default discount %.
- CRM: contact log (append-only), follow-ups (manual or seasonal-suggested — seasonal not wired yet), quotes (draft→sent→won→order), analytics (LTV, repeat, AOV).

## 3. Pricing hierarchy (top wins)
1. Per-customer, per-product fixed override
2. Per-customer, per-product % discount *(proposed, not yet built)*
3. Per-customer, per-collection % discount *(proposed, not yet built)*
4. Variant × channel override price
5. Variant default price

Prices stored gross; per-variant VAT rate (Austria food = 10%); net derived on display.

## 4. Sales channels
| Channel | Order arrival | Cadence | Quirks |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Online shop** | CSV import (Shopify etc.) | ~every 2 days | `sourceRef` dedup |
| **Shop floor** | POS + weekly count upload | Weekly reconcile | Auto-replenish on min; can **borrow** to serve incoming B2B if replenishment can finish before next opening |
| **Events** | Manual (event name req.) | Pre-planned per calendar | **Packed-and-ready deadline BEFORE event day** |
| **B2B** | Manual, often via Quote | Per-customer deadline | Often branded/custom packaging + custom prices |

Every order: channel, customer, deadline, priority, delivery method. Every line: product (or variant-expanded), qty, unit price, VAT rate, fulfilment mode (`produce` or `borrow`).

## 5. Order → Production end-to-end
```
Customer buys → Order saved → Regenerate click → Batches grouped → Scheduler assigns days → 9 production steps → Stock moves → Packing → Done
```

### Scheduling rules (the important part)
- Batches are created/rescheduled **only** by user clicking Regenerate.
- Reconciler: one draft batch per productId; multiple orders for same product SHARE a batch.
- Batch effective deadline = min(deadline of any linked order).
- `daysToDeadline ≤ mergingWindowDays` (default 14) → **forward mode** (start today, pack forward).
- Else → **reverse mode**: anchor at `deadline − productionBufferDays` (default 2 working days), walk backward.
- Capacity = sum of available people-minutes/day; waiting/drying minutes don't count.
- Mould occupancy hard-locked step 1→7.
- `planStepStatus.done=true` = session lock; regenerate respects it.
- Only `draft` batches rebuild. Active/done batches frozen.
- **Shelf life NOT considered.** Known gap — can schedule too early for long-deadline orders.

### 9 production steps
| # | Step | Stock effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Polishing | — |
| 2 | Painting | — |
| 3 | Shelling | **Ingredient grams −** |
| 4 | Filling Prep | — (sources filling stock) |
| 5 | Filling | **Filling grams −** |
| 6 | Capping | — |
| 7 | Unmoulding | **Pieces + (Production Storage)**; moulds freed; waste logged |
| 8 | Transfer | Stock movement to Store/Freezer/Allocated |
| 9 | Packing | **Packaging units −** |

Bars skip 1–2. Drying times are **info notes only**. No countdown timers.

## 6. Stock — two parallel systems

### Pieces (products) — 4 locations
```
Production Storage ← unmould default
       ↓↑
     Store          ← walk-in customers, per-product min
       ↓↑
    Freezer         ← frozenAt/defrostedAt, preserved shelf life
       ↓
   Allocated        ← reserved for specific order
```
- Every move logged: reason/date/who/qty.
- FIFO across batches.
- Per-product per-location min → low-stock alerts + auto-add to shopping list.

### Grams / units (raw materials)
- **Ingredients**: per-ingredient g + min threshold; movements on Shelling + Filling Prep.
- **Fillings**: per-filling g + made-at date + freeze status.
- **Packaging**: per-unit qty; deducted on Packing; lead-time aware.

### Inventory counts
- Weekly physical count per location → variance + reason (sold/waste/breakage/unknown).
- Variance report surfaces consistent losses.

## 7. People & capacity
- Per person: roles, working days, default hours/day, start/end time, unavailability windows.
- Workshop-wide config: warn%, critical%, fillingBufferPercent, labourHourlyRate (€/h), productionBufferDays, mergingWindowWeeks, stockExpiryWarnDays.
- Equipment: 2× Selmi PlusEx, 1× Selmi small, 1× Royal Catering, 2+1 Chocolate World melters, 1× Selmi R200 belt. Tempering allocated per step; conflict alerts.

## 8. HACCP / compliance
- **Daily temperature log** on opening; pre-filled with yesterday's values; out-of-range → required note; exportable CSV/PDF. Defaults: Fridge 2–5 °C, Freezer ≤ −18 °C, Chocolate storage 16–18 °C.
- **Close Production** scans unfinished steps → rolls to tomorrow → scheduler recomputes deadline slippage → opens cleaning checklist.
- **Cleaning checklist UI NOT YET BUILT** (schema reserved, table skipped).
- **Traceability**: batchNumber `DUL-YYYYMMDD-NN` + batchSummary (exact recipe + filling version) + waste log + temp readings → recall can reconstruct every piece.

## 9. Events & calendar
- Event calendar entries types: event / peak / blocked / holiday.
- Scheduler skips blocked; highlights peaks.
- Shop opening hours + closures drive the borrow-or-produce decision.

## 10. Business intelligence
- **Dashboard**: today's batches, open orders by urgency, low-stock alerts, capacity %.
- **Pricing & Margins**: variant margin health, avg product cost, box price vs cost.
- **Production Stats**: output by product/variant, trends.
- **Product Cost**: full breakdown per product (shell/fillings/decoration/overhead).
- **Customer analytics**: LTV, AOV, repeat, last order.
- **Stock reports**: low-stock, oldest, waste reasons.

## 11. Cross-process connections
```
Ingredient stock ── deducted on ─→ Shelling, Filling Prep
Filling stock    ── deducted on ─→ Filling; replenished after Filling Prep
Packaging stock  ── deducted on ─→ Packing
Product pieces   ── created on ──→ Unmoulding → moved by Transfer → reserved in Allocated

Moulds pool      ── locked by ──→ batch steps 1–7
                 ── freed by ──→ Unmoulding → alerts "N moulds just freed"
People calendar  ── drives ──→ daily capacity minutes
Equipment        ── allocated by ──→ Shelling, Capping

Orders           ── group by productId ──→ Batches (Reconciler)
Batches          ── scheduled using ──→ deadlines + capacity + moulds + equipment
Unmould waste    ── triggers ──→ "can order still be fulfilled?" alert
Order items      ── fulfilled from ──→ Allocated (produce) OR Store (borrow)
Shop opening     ── drives ──→ borrow-or-produce decision

Weekly filling consolidation ── reads ALL open orders → sums → buffer → − stock → cooking list
HACCP temp log   ── required on ──→ every production-day open
Cleaning checklist ── required on ──→ every production-day close

Quotes           ── won converts to ──→ Order
B2B feasibility  ── reads ──→ capacity config + current schedule load
```

## 13. Founder's one-page narrative
> Ingredients → Fillings → Products → Variants (SKUs) → Collections (labels). Four channels — shop / online / events / B2B — at channel-specific prices to labelled customer segments.
>
> Orders save. Nothing is produced yet. Regenerate groups open orders by product into one batch each, and plans them backward from deadlines across the calendar — respecting moulds, people, blocked days, and started work.
>
> Each batch walks ≤ 9 steps (polishing → painting → shelling → filling → capping → unmoulding → transfer → packing), decrementing ingredient/filling/packaging stock, freeing moulds at the end, landing pieces in Production → Store / Freezer / Allocated. Every step logged for traceability.
>
> Daily: open production (temp check required) → run batches → close production (cleaning checklist required).
>
> On top: two parallel stock systems (pieces / grams), FIFO + shelf-life + weekly recount. CRM around B2B (quotes, follow-ups, pricing hierarchy). Events pre-plan the calendar. Equipment + people set capacity ceiling.
>
> The system's job: keep the whole web consistent and honest, warn Manuela the moment any part drifts out of feasibility.
